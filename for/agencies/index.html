Vynix for agencies

Vynix helps web and design agencies collect clear client feedback directly on staging and live sites. Clients click what is wrong, and your team gets the context needed to fix it without a long email thread.

The problem

Agency feedback often arrives as screenshots, vague comments, and messages spread across email, docs, chat, and calls. Developers then have to recreate the issue, inspect the page, ask for browser details, and guess what the client meant. This slows review rounds and turns small fixes into project drag.

Collect feedback where the work actually lives

Add the Vynix widget to a staging site or live site, then let clients click on the exact part of the page they want to discuss. Their note stays tied to the page and element, so your team is not matching comments to screenshots after the fact.

Use review rounds, projects, roles, and sharing to keep feedback organized across clients, sites, and internal teams.

Give developers the context they usually have to chase

When someone annotates a page, Vynix captures the selected element, a screenshot, console context, network context, and an AI diagnosis of the likely root cause. That gives developers a clearer starting point than a comment like "this looks broken on my laptop."

The result is less back-and-forth with account managers and clients, and fewer fixes blocked by missing technical details.

Turn approved feedback into build-ready tasks

Once an issue is ready for development, your team can copy a ready-to-build prompt or open a GitHub issue and assign it to a coding agent. The handoff includes the captured context, so implementation starts closer to the actual problem.

Vynix fits the agency workflow between client review and production work, helping feedback move from comment to fix without being rewritten several times.

What you get

  • Click-to-annotate any page so clients can point at the exact text, layout, image, or interaction they mean.
  • Automatic developer context captures the element, screenshot, console details, and network details with each annotation.
  • AI root-cause diagnosis gives your team a likely starting point before anyone opens devtools.
  • Hand off to a coding agent by copying a ready-to-build prompt or opening a GitHub issue.
  • Review rounds, projects, roles, and sharing keep client feedback organized across agency teams and client stakeholders.
